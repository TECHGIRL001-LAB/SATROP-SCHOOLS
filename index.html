SATROP SCHOOLS

Brighter Tomorrow is Our Promise

SATROP SCHOOLS is a government approved school providing excellent foundational education for children from early years to Key Stage 2. We welcome children from 3 months to 5 years for Creche and Pre-school education, focusing on the Montessori Method of learning, while children from 6 years to 11 years of age are offered an excellent primary education based on integrated British and Nigeria curricula. Our class sizes are small, with one or two classes per year group – and we take great care to encourage and stretch children based on their individual needs and strengths.

Curriculum

SATROP Schools offer a broad, integrated curriculum combining the British and Nigerian curricula. In our primary school, pupils develop essential academic skills through engaging activities that promote communication, collaboration, creativity, and critical thinking across subjects such as Literacy, Numeracy, Science, ICT, Art, French, and Social Studies. In the Early Years, we follow the Montessori method, using hands-on learning to build knowledge, skills, and curiosity across key developmental areas. Supported by trips and projects, our curriculum encourages exploration, lateral thinking, and a genuine interest in the world.

Learning Support

All children need learning support at different stages. Our Learning Support Department works with teachers to adapt the curriculum, provide appropriate resources, and support individual learning needs through assessments and collaboration with staff and parents. Academic clinics help pupils overcome challenges, while our lessons focus on building confidence and independence.
